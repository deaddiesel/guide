§6Chapter 7: In-World Projection

§7In this chapter, you will learn how to use the projection system for step-by-step construction of complex structures right inside your game world.

§bActivating the Projection

§7If the opened guide chapter contains a 3D structure model, the §6[Projection]§7 button will become available in the bottom-left corner of the interface.

§7Step-by-step process:
§61. §7Stand on a flat area where you plan to start building.
§62. §7Open the required page in the guidebook and click §6[Projection]§7.
§63. §7You will be automatically exited from the book interface, and a full-sized, translucent silhouette of the future structure will deploy in front of you.
§64. §7A Sci-Fi panel for precise shifting and positioning will instantly appear on your screen.

§l§6━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

§bControl and Positioning Panel

§7A navigation console will open on the left, right below the boss bar. All button clicks are accompanied by high-frequency sculk-sensor sounds.

§7Available tools:
§6• [ Left ] [ Right ] §7— Shifts the structure horizontally left and right.
§6• [ Down ] [ Up ] §7— Displaces the structure vertically down and up.
§6• [ Back ] [ Forward ] §7— Moves the structure horizontally backward and forward.
§6• [ - 90° ] [ + 90° ] §7— Rotates the projection around its central axis by 90 degrees.

§7Final buttons:
§6• [ Done ] §7— Locks the blueprint in the world, closes the panel, and starts the construction.
§6• [ Cancel ] §7— Fully collapses the projector, wipes the silhouette, and returns to the game.

§l§6━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

§bBuilding Process and Indicators

§7After clicking the §aDone§7 button, the projection locks in space. An intelligent monitoring system for the construction site is activated.

§7Tracking mechanics:

§71. §lSmart Boss Bar (Progress Indicator)
§7A bar will appear at the top of the screen, displaying the exact number of correctly placed blocks. It dynamically changes color:
§6• §dPink §7— Initial construction stage (up to 40% completion)
§6• §3Blue §7— Main assembly stage (from 40% to 90%)
§6• §aGreen §7— Final stage of structure completion (from 90%)

§72. §lAutomatic Block Hiding
§7As soon as you place the correct block in the correct spot, its translucent silhouette §6instantly disappears§7 from the projection. This clears your view and shows you where to move next.

§73. §lError Highlighting (Emergency Outline)
§7If you place an incorrect block (e.g., cobblestone instead of an iron block), the system will temporarily freeze the progress calculation. A §cbright red 3D wireframe box§7 will light up around the wrong block. The block remains perfectly visible through it, allowing you to quickly fix the mistake.

§l§6━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

§bAccidental Removal Protection

§7If you accidentally press §7[§6Shift + Right Click§7] on the hologram or air while building, the blueprint §cwill not§7 be wiped.

§7The built-in protection system will trigger:
§7• A deep bass warning sound from a note block will play.
§7• A notification will appear: §7[§6!§7] Press [§6Shift + Right Click§7] again.
§7• If you press it a second time within 3 seconds, the projection will close. If not, the timer resets, safely preserving your progress.

§bCompletion of Construction

§a★ Structure fully assembled! ★

§7The moment the very last block of the mechanism is placed:
§6• §7The progress indicator will fill up to §a100%§7.
§6• §7A powerful, triumphant Sci-Fi chord will echo.
§6• §7A festive §bfountain of cyan soul flames§7 and §egolden totem sparks§7 will burst around your finished build.
§6• §7After 3 seconds, the projection will automatically shut down, leaving you in front of a completely finished, working structure.
